About Go Fresh

Go Fresh started as a weekend farmers' market in 2019. Today we partner with over 120 smallholder farms across the region to bring seasonal, pesticide-free produce directly to your doorstep. Our mission is to make healthy eating simple, affordable and sustainable.

What we believe
  • Transparent sourcing — we publish farm profiles and harvest dates.
  • Reduce waste — we package minimally and compost where possible.
  • Support local economies — most of our farmers are family-owned businesses.
